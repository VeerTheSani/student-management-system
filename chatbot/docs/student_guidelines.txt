GANDHINAGAR UNIVERSITY - BCA STUDENT GUIDELINES 2024-25

ATTENDANCE POLICY:
- Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory in each subject.
- Below 75% attendance = not allowed to sit in end semester exam.
- Medical leave requires doctor certificate submitted within 3 days.
- Attendance is marked at start of every lecture. Late entry counts as absent.

GRADING SYSTEM:
O  (Outstanding)  - 90-100 marks - 10 grade points
A+ (Excellent)    - 80-89 marks  - 9 grade points
A  (Very Good)    - 70-79 marks  - 8 grade points
B+ (Good)         - 60-69 marks  - 7 grade points
B  (Above Average)- 55-59 marks  - 6 grade points
C  (Average)      - 50-54 marks  - 5 grade points
F  (Fail)         - Below 50     - 0 grade points

CGPA CALCULATION:
CGPA = Sum of (Grade Points x Credits) / Total Credits

BACKLOG POLICY:
- Students with F grade must appear in backlog exam.
- Backlog exam held in June every year.
- Maximum 3 attempts allowed per subject.
- Backlog fee: Rs. 500 per subject.

LIBRARY RULES:
- Library timing: 9 AM to 5 PM (Monday to Saturday)
- Maximum 3 books can be issued at a time.
- Book return period: 14 days. Fine: Rs. 2 per day after due date.

LAB RULES:
- Lab timing: As per timetable only.
- Personal laptops allowed with prior permission.
- No food or drinks inside lab.
- Students responsible for any damage to equipment.
